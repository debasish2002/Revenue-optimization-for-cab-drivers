# Revenue-optimization-for-cab-drivers

- Analyzed payment behaviors across 6M+ taxi trips to investigate revenue drivers.
- Conducted A/B testing and t-tests to validate the hypothesis that credit card payments lead to significantly higher
fare amounts than cash.
- Designed multipage dashboards with slicers and filters, helping stakeholders drill down by region, product category,
and time.
- Delivered data-backed recommendations for driver incentives, projecting a potential revenue increase of 15%.
